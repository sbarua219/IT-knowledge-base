# Environmental Impact of Technology

## The Hidden Infrastructure
Technology relies on physical systems such as:
- Data centers
- Servers
- Cooling systems
- Continuous power supply

## Impact of Modern Technology
As demand for cloud computing and AI increases, so does energy consumption.

## Trade-Offs
- Increased efficiency through automation
- Increased resource usage behind the scenes

## Organizational Considerations
- Energy-efficient systems
- Optimized workloads
- Sustainable infrastructure planning

## Real-World Insight
Digital systems may feel invisible, but they have real-world environmental costs that organizations are beginning to consider more seriously.
