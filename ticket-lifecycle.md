# Ticket Lifecycle in IT Support

## What is a Ticket?
A ticket represents a reported issue or request submitted by a user.

## Typical Lifecycle
1. Ticket is created
2. Assigned to a support team
3. Investigated and worked on
4. Resolved and closed

## Where Delays Happen
- High ticket volume
- Poor prioritization
- Lack of clear ownership

## Real-World Insight
In enterprise environments, tickets are rarely simple. Multiple dependencies, approvals, and system limitations can slow down resolution even when teams are working efficiently.
