# AI in IT Environments

## Role of AI in IT
AI is used to automate tasks, analyze data, and assist with decision-making in IT systems.

## Benefits
- Faster issue detection
- Automation of repetitive tasks
- Improved system monitoring

## Limitations
- Lack of real-world context
- Potential for incorrect outputs
- Overreliance by users

## Risks
- AI-generated phishing attacks
- Misuse of automation
- Data privacy concerns

## Real-World Insight
AI is a powerful tool, but it requires human oversight to be effective and reliable.
