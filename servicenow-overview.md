# ServiceNow Overview in Enterprise IT

## What is ServiceNow?
ServiceNow is a platform used to manage IT services, workflows, and support operations within organizations.

## Core Functions
- Incident Management
- Request Fulfillment
- Problem Management
- Change Management

## Why It Matters
ServiceNow centralizes IT operations, allowing teams to track, manage, and resolve issues efficiently.

## Common Challenges
- Poor configuration leading to inefficiencies
- Overloaded ticket queues
- Lack of user understanding

## Real-World Insight
ServiceNow is only as effective as the processes behind it. Without proper structure, it can become a bottleneck instead of a solution.
