# Global IT Operations

## What Are Global IT Operations?
Global IT operations support systems, users, and infrastructure across multiple regions and time zones.

## Key Challenges
- Time zone differences
- Communication delays
- Regional compliance requirements
- Infrastructure variability

## Why It Matters
Organizations operate 24/7, requiring IT systems to remain stable and responsive across the world.

## Common Issues
- Misaligned priorities between regions
- Delayed incident responses
- Lack of real-time coordination

## Real-World Insight
Supporting global systems requires more than technical skill—it requires awareness of how systems behave across different environments and user expectations.
